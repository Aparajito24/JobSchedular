Job Timeline


  • Verification
  • Eligible

Verification

The candidate must fullfil the requirements of the company. Candidate must verify all the details from the Verification section.

Eligible

After the verification the candidate is ready for the tests conducted by the company. Candidate is verified with all his/her details and is eligible to apply for the test.

Test

The candidate must score atleast the cut-off of the company. If the Candidate scores the minimum marks, if so he/she is selected.

Technical Interview

After clearing the test the candidate will sit for the Technical Interview and questions will be asked as per companies criteria.

HR Interview

After clearing the Technical Interview the candidate will sit for the HR Interview and questions will be asked by the HR.If the candidate is able to clear the HR interview he or she will get a job letter if selected.
